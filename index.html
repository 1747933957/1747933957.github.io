About

Welcome to my personal homepage. This page is designed to present my academic interests, educational background, publications, and internships.

I am a Ph.D. candidate at Nanjing University, advised by Prof. Sheng Zhang. My research interests include computer vision, computer networks, autonomous driving, and video analytics. I am particularly interested in efficient visual intelligence systems, collaborative perception, and real-time analysis and transmission of visual data in practical environments.
